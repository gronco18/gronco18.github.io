PolyHack Projectknowyourrights.me

During the Tufts hackathon, Polyhack, my team and I created a website that acts as a comprehensive resource for survivors of sexual misconduct. We ended up being finalists, but what felt most like an accomplishment was making something that was actually doing good. Find more about it here.

Jumbocode Projects: Web Dev & Data Vis

In Jumbocode, we code applications for non-profit organizations. During the past two semesters, I worked with teams on a website for Save the Children which allowed sponsors of children around the world to more easily (and thus more often) interact with the children they sponsored.
This semester, my team and I are working on data visualizations for herpetological data for the NYHerpAtlas and UpstreamTech. Both of these projects are super cool, but are on private github repos. You can check Jumbocode out on Facebook, though.
