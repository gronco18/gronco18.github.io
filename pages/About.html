Hi! I'm Gabriella.

I am a student at Tufts University in Medford, MA studying Computer Science and Political Science. I am originally from CT, though I spend most of my time outside of school on my grandmother's farm in WV. Some of my hobbies include writing, photography, running, hiking and gardening.

I am particularly interested in cyber security, data and privacy policies. In my interdisciplinary studies, I hope to learn more about how people interact with technology and how technology impacts society. Further, as a political science major, I am interested in understanding digital strategies for both campaigns and companies.
